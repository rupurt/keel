**Keel introduces Domain Driven Design Restructure: Restructure keel into explicit DDD bounded contexts and layered architecture so subsystems can evolve independently and in parallel with verifiable contracts.**

**LUNAR BASE ALPHA** — Keel today announced Domain Driven Design Restructure, a major leap forward in agentic SDLC management.

### The Problem
Teams lose delivery confidence when planning, execution, and verification are disconnected and drift over time.

### The Solution
Domain Driven Design Restructure creates a coherent path from planning artifacts to completed work so teams can move faster with consistent quality gates.

### Key Features
- **Coherent planning artifacts**: Requirements and design stay aligned with execution scope.
- **Guardrailed delivery flow**: Shared validation and state transitions enforce consistent quality.
- **Clear verification trail**: Evidence and outcomes remain traceable from story to voyage.
