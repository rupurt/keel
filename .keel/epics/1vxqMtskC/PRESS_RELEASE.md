**LUNAR BASE ALPHA** — Keel today announced Automated Verification Techniques Bank, a major leap forward in agentic SDLC management.

### The Solution
Automated Verification Techniques Bank creates a coherent path from planning artifacts to completed work so teams can move faster with consistent quality gates.

- **Coherent planning artifacts**: Requirements and design stay aligned with execution scope.
- **Guardrailed delivery flow**: Shared validation and state transitions enforce consistent quality.
- **Clear verification trail**: Evidence and outcomes remain traceable from story to voyage.
